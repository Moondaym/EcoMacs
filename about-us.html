Our Story

Back in 2009, our founder Yousofe Osama was a tech enthusiast and a broke college student who needed a reliable laptop for school—but the prices were outrageous. After hunting for a more affordable MacBook, they realized that high-quality, refurbished devices weren't just a smart choice; they were the future of sustainable and budget-friendly tech. What started as a side hustle—buying, restoring, and reselling MacBooks from a tiny dorm room—quickly grew into something bigger. More and more people wanted premium Apple devices without the premium price tag, and we knew we were onto something. Today, EcoMacs is built on that same mission: to make top-tier technology accessible and affordable. Every MacBook we sell is professionally refurbished, rigorously tested, and backed by a solid warranty—so you can enjoy Apple quality without the Apple price. Join us in redefining what it means to buy tech. Better for your wallet. Better for the planet.
